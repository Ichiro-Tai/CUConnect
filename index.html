Our Mission

We aim to create a platform used to bring the community of Urbana-Champaign into a mutually beneficial and self-sustaining relationship. Using our cutting-edge interactive educational tool CANVASTM, we provide an abundance of part-time jobs for talent at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign to act as affordable tutors for local families, while families reward them for their effort by paying for coupons to be used in various local businesses to boost the economy.
